A Note from the Creator

Hey, welcome!

Thanks for giving Flash Dash a try.

I built it because I kept opening a new tab and feeling... nothing. Just empty space where something useful could be. So I created the new tab I always wished I had: a clean clock, quick search, a space for your goals and photos, your tasks, and your bookmarks, all together in one calm, clutter-free home screen.

What started as a small idea went through countless iterations and ended up teaching me a lot about the kind of product I wanted to build. Every detail you see here, from the animations and themes to the little finishing touches, was crafted one late night at a time.

I hope Flash Dash makes your everyday feel a little more focused, a little more organized, and maybe even a little more yours.

Thanks for being here from the beginning. It truly means a lot.

cheers, aryan
